Bootstrap 3 - Jumbotron

Jumbotron

A lightweight, flexible component that can optionally extend the entire viewport to showcase key marketing messages on your site.

Default display is a grey box with rounded corners

Font sizes are enlarged inside the Jumbotron area.

Jumbotron Outside Container

When the jumbotron is placed outside the <div class="container"> it will extend the jumbotron area to the screen edges

Page Header Lives Here

The .page-header class adds a horizontal line under the heading

It also adds some extra space around the heading element.
